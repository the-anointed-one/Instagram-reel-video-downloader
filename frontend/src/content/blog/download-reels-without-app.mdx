## The Short Answer: Yes

You don't need to install any app to download Instagram Reels. Browser-based tools like [ReelFetch](/) let you paste a Reel link and get an instant MP4 download — from any device, any browser.

## Why Browser-Based Is Better

### 1. No App Store Bloat

Reel downloader apps on the App Store and Play Store are notorious for:

- Excessive permissions (contacts, storage, camera)
- Aggressive ad networks
- Background data collection
- Taking up phone storage

A browser-based tool needs **zero permissions** and leaves nothing on your device.

### 2. No Account Required

Many apps ask you to sign in with your Instagram account. This is a major security risk — you're handing your credentials to a third-party app. **ReelFetch never asks for login credentials.** It only processes publicly available URLs.

### 3. Works Everywhere

Apps are platform-locked. A browser tool works on:

- iPhone Safari
- Android Chrome
- Windows / Mac desktop
- Tablets
- Even smart TVs with browsers

### 4. Always Up to Date

When Instagram changes its page structure (which happens often), mobile apps need an update from the app store. Browser tools can adapt instantly on the server side, with no action needed from you.
