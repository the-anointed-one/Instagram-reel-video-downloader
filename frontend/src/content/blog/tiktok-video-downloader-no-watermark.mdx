## Why TikTok Videos Have Watermarks

TikTok adds watermarks to all videos to:
- Discourage downloading and sharing outside the TikTok platform
- Keep their branding visible whenever TikTok content spreads online
- Attribute videos back to creators when shared elsewhere

The watermark is embedded directly in the video — it's not just a banner you can crop out. It follows the content.

Most download tools simply grab the same watermarked version TikTok serves publicly. ReelFetch uses a different approach — fetching the clean source file via TikTok's own API before the watermark is applied — resulting in a watermark-free MP4.
