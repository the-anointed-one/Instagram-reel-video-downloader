## Saving Your Own Reels to Your Phone

If the Reel is one you posted yourself, Instagram has a built-in option to save it to your phone's camera roll.

1. Open the **Instagram app**
2. Go to your **profile** and tap the Reel
3. Tap the **three-dot menu** (⋯) in the corner
4. Tap **Save to camera roll**

That's it — the video downloads directly to your Photos (iPhone) or Gallery (Android).

**Limitation:** This only works for Reels you personally posted. Instagram does not allow you to download other people's Reels natively.

Yes — tested and working on iPhone with iOS 16, 17, and 18.

When you tap Download MP4 in Safari on iPhone, the video saves to your **Files app** first (in the Downloads folder).

To move it to Photos:

2. Navigate to **Downloads**
3. Tap the video → tap the **Share icon** → **Save Video**

It's now in your iPhone camera roll permanently.
