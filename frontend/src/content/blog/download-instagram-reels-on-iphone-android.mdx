## Can You Download Reels on Your Phone?

Yes — and you don't need to install an app to do it. Most "Reels download" apps on the App Store and Play Store are loaded with ads and unnecessary permissions. A browser-based tool like [ReelFetch](/) works directly from Safari or Chrome on your phone.

## Download Instagram Reels on iPhone

### Step 1: Copy the Reel Link

1. Open the Instagram app
2. Find the Reel you want to save
3. Tap the **share** button (paper plane icon)
4. Tap **Copy Link**

### Step 2: Open ReelFetch in Safari

Open Safari and go to [ReelFetch](/). Paste the link into the input field and tap **Fetch Reel**.

### Step 3: Download the Video

Once the video preview appears, tap the **Download MP4** button. Safari will ask where to save the file — choose "Downloads" or your preferred location.

**Finding the file:** Open the Files app → Browse → Downloads. The video will be there as an `.mp4` file.

### Save to Camera Roll

Want the Reel in your Photos app? After downloading:

1. Open **Files** app
2. Find the downloaded `.mp4`
3. Tap and hold → **Share** → **Save Video**

The Reel is now in your Camera Roll. For a more detailed walkthrough, see our [Save Reels to Camera Roll guide](/blog/save-instagram-reels-to-camera-roll).

## Download Instagram Reels on Android

### Step 1: Copy the Reel Link

Same as iPhone — open the Reel in the Instagram app, tap share, then **Copy Link**.

### Step 2: Open ReelFetch in Chrome

Go to [ReelFetch](/) in Chrome. Paste the URL and tap **Fetch Reel**.

### Step 3: Download

Tap **Download MP4**. Chrome will download the file to your Downloads folder automatically.

**Finding the file:** Open your file manager → Downloads. The `.mp4` file will be there, ready to play or share.
