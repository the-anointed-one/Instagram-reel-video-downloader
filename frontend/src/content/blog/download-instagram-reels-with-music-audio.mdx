## Why Do Some Tools Strip Audio?

Some Reels download tools strip audio because:

1. **Re-encoding** — they convert the video to a different format, and the audio gets lost
2. **Separate streams** — Instagram sometimes serves video and audio as separate DASH streams; cheap tools only grab the video
3. **Copyright filtering** — some tools remove copyrighted music to avoid DMCA issues

ReelFetch avoids all of these problems by downloading the **progressive MP4** — the single file that combines video and audio together.
