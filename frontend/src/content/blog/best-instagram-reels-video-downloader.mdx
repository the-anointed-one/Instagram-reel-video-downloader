## Why So Many Reels Downloaders?

Instagram doesn't offer a built-in download button for Reels. That gap has spawned hundreds of "Instagram Reels video downloader" tools — apps, websites, browser extensions, and Telegram bots. But most are terrible: bloated with ads, riddled with pop-ups, or outright dangerous.

We tested the most popular options and compared them on what actually matters.

## What We Tested

We evaluated each tool on:

- **Speed** — how long from pasting a URL to getting a download link
- **Ads / pop-ups** — intrusive advertising, redirect chains
- **Privacy** — login requirements, data collection, permissions
- **Mobile support** — does it work on iPhone and Android browsers?
- **Reliability** — does it actually work with current Instagram pages?

**Best for:** Users who prefer native apps and don't mind ads.

### 5. Browser Extensions

| Criteria | Rating |
|---|---|
| Speed | Fast (in-page button) |
| Ads | Usually none |
| Login required | You must be logged into Instagram |
| Mobile support | Desktop only |
| Reliability | Breaks with Instagram updates |

Browser extensions inject a download button directly on Instagram pages. Convenient, but they require you to be logged in, only work on desktop, and frequently break when Instagram updates their frontend.

**Best for:** Desktop users who want one-click downloads from the Instagram website.
